About the Event

Enter the world of ethical hacking in our Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. You'll face a series of challenges across various domains like forensics, cryptography, web exploitation, and reverse engineering. Your task is to find the hidden "flag" in each challenge to score points. This is your chance to think like a hacker and defend like a pro.

Rules and Regulations

  • This is a "Jeopardy-style" CTF where you can choose challenges from different categories.
  • Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to 2.
  • Attacking the competition infrastructure or other teams is strictly prohibited.
  • Points are awarded for each flag captured, with harder challenges worth more points.
  • Hints may be released for certain challenges throughout the event.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Hak5 WiFi Pineapple & 1-Year Hack The Box VIP+ Subscription.
  • 2nd Place: Flipper Zero & Exclusive CTF Winner T-Shirt.
  • 3rd Place: 1-Year TryHackMe Premium & Certificate of Prowess.
