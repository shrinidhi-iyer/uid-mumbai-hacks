About Mumbai Hacks

Sackful of Hacks

Mumbai Hacks is more than just a competition; it's a celebration of innovation, problem-solving, and the vibrant spirit of the developer community. Our mission is to provide a platform for aspiring programmers, seasoned developers, and tech enthusiasts to come together, test their skills, and push the boundaries of what's possible.

Join us for a series of challenging events, insightful workshops, and networking opportunities that will leave you inspired and better equipped for the future of technology.
